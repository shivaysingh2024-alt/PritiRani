❤️ Love Story

Har khubsurat kahani ek muskaan se shuru hoti hai…

Photos mein dikhne wale har pal se ek baat saaf nazar aati hai—tumhare mummy aur papa ek dusre ke saath khush rehte the. Anniversary ki photo ho, Holi ka din ho ya kisi function ki tasveer, unke chehre ki muskaan unke pyaar aur apnapan ko dikhati hai.

Tumhari mummy ne sirf ek wife ka farz nahi nibhaya, balki har mushkil mein apne jeevan saathi ka haath thaam kar saath diya.

💔 The Difficult Journey

2023 mein jab papa ki tabiyat achanak kharab hui, tab hamari zindagi badal gayi.

Baad mein doctors ne bataya ki papa ko Brain Tumour tha. Papa ko apni bimari ki poori sachchai ka pata nahi tha. Lekin mummy ko sab pata tha…

Fir bhi unhone kabhi papa ko udaas nahi hone diya.

🕊 15 June 2024

Ye hamari zindagi ka sabse dard bhara din tha.

15 June 2024… Papa hamesha ke liye hume chhod kar chale gaye.

Us din mummy toot gayi… Lekin sirf andar se.

Bahar se woh fir bhi hamare liye mazboot bani rahi.

🌺 A Brave Mother

Aaj bhi mummy…

Subah sabse pehle uthti hain.
Hamare future ke baare mein sochti hain.
Hamari padhai sambhalti hain.
Har zarurat poori karti hain.
Aur kabhi mehsoos nahi hone deti ki hamne kitna bada nuksan dekha hai.

Yehi hoti hai…

A Real Mother.
